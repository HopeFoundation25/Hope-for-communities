About Us

Hope for Communities Foundation is a nonprofit organization committed to mentoring boys aged 9–17 and empowering caregivers in low-income communities through savings and economic initiatives like Village Savings and Loan Associations (VSLAs).

Our Programs

  • Boys Mentorship Program: Life skills, character building, and personal development for boys aged 9–17.
  • VSLA Initiatives: Financial empowerment programs for caregivers and guardians through group savings and lending.
  • Community Empowerment: Education, advocacy, and community training sessions on parenting, addiction, and youth development.
